Knowledge Server
Topic Maps and K42 provide a collaborative environment for capturing, expressing and delivering knowledge.
They will enable organisations to access their greatest and most valuable asset - their knowledge.
More and more companies, organisations and communities are realising that information and knowledge are key to operating and competing in their respective marketplaces. With the huge amounts of data being created and stored the ability to effectively search, navigate and retrieve the inherent knowledge represented by that data has become crucial.
"How effectively organizations deal with mission-critical information and expose it as usable content to support employees, partners, and consumers is becoming a recognized differentiator. This will create a competitive rift or information gap based on organizations' ability to deliver timely access to the relevant business content."
A.Warzecha: "Electronic Business Strategies" Meta Group 23/01/2001 copyright © Meta Group inc.

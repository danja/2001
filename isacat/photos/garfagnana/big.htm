Garfagnana Photos

Some typical scenes of the Garfagna valley, between the Apuane Alps and the Apennines in the north of Italy. I'm told Garfagna means 'big big forest' though it's not in my dictionary. There are an awful lot of chestnut trees around though.

 

Gragnanella - typical pretty hill village (note the anachronistic telegraph wires - everyone in the region has cellphones now).

 

Carvings above a church door in Castiglione. Above are what look like remnants of a fresco.

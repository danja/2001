Ask at the Post Office

Mailing list with a difference. When you subscribe, you provide a list of keywords that describe your areas of knowledge. When someone posts a query to the list, only the people whose keywords match those of the query receive the mail. When anyone replies to a post, the keywords in the reply are added to that persons list (with added priority?) .

Probably needs some way of increasing the 'experts' list.

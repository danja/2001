Here are a couple of the rules from the knowledgebase.

The first rule has as its consequent the alive fact and as its condition the Is it alive question.
Ok, so its pretty redundant to have a rule that says If its alive then its alive, but remember this is a human interpretation of the text - as far as the system will be concerned Is it alive and alive are two completely different facts, and their only relationship is that defined by the rule.

The second rule here represents IF NOT alive AND can it fly THEN Helicopter, which is logically the same as our experts knowledge that a helicopter can fly but isn't alive.

Mudman Spec

I use this term to describe a specification that is not structurally sound but might have a form that could be usable with a little squishing.

Not far off the American slang 'strawman', but I don't like this term as the root English equivalent 'scarecrow' carries nothing like the same connotations. I'm thinking that horror film with the crap lion in it, and a bloke that looks like a bucket. I believe a strawman in this context was originally a candidate that was put up for political office with the prior knowledge that they wouldn't stand a chance. A scarecrow stands in a field, errm, scaring crows.

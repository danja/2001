The Randomba Inn is situated on the beautiful South West coast of SriLanka.

The clean and comfortable accommodation is situated right next to a pristine beach, which more often than not you have to yourself.

The Randomba Inn is a family run concern, the proprietors being Mr.and Mrs Silva.
Mr. Silva speaks excellent English and can offer you a wealth of information on Sri Lankan culture and interesting places to visit.

Mrs Silva provides good breakfasts (75r) and delicious traditonal Sri Lankan evening meals(150r) on request.

The Inn has four double rooms available and the cost is 600 rupees per double room per night.

When you want a break from swimming in the sea and relaxing on the beach there are temples, lagoons and a spice island to explore. You can release turtles into the sea at Kosgoda and nearby Ambalangoda is famous for its mask carvers, traditional dance school and batiks.
The snorkeling on the coral reef at Hikkaduwa is an experience of a lifetime.

How to find us.
Approaching Ambalangoda from Colombo direction, Randomba Inn is about one and a half kilometers before Ambalangoda town centre (bus stand). A 3 wheeler from Ambalangoda will cost 50r.

A possible itinerary around Sri Lanka could be (anti clockwise round the island) coral diving at Hikkaduwa and Unawatuna, The Yala Park Nature Reserve, The Sinharaja Rainforest, The tea plantations, Kandy and The Temple of the Tooth , Temple caves at Dambulla, Sigiriya Rock Fortress and the ancient city of Polonnaruwa (monkeys and temples and Anuradhapura(archeology).After returning to Kandy you could visit The Elephant Orphanage at Kegalle and the canal system, fishing boats and lagoons of Negombo.

You can find more information about Sri Lanka from the Ceylon Tourist Board on www.lanka.net/ctb/ and the Lonely Planet Guide www.lonelyplanet.com
